Important Notice

Since educational content is delivered digitally and our courses are free, there are no cancellations for course access. This policy applies specifically to donations.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund for your donation, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to contactedusankalp@gmail.com with the subject "Refund Request"
  2. Include your full name and the blockchain transaction hash (TXID) from your donation
  3. Provide your wallet address for the refund
  4. Mention the reason for your refund request (optional)
  5. We will process your request within 24-48 hours

Blockchain Payment Processing

All donations are processed through our secure blockchain payment system. Refunds will be issued to the original wallet address used during the donation process. Please note that blockchain network fees (gas fees) cannot be refunded as they are paid to network validators, not to EduSankalp.
